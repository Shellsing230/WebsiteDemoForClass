About Me!

Thanks for visiting my page. A bit about me. I was born and raised in southern California. I joined the Air Force Reserves right out of high school and served for 6 years as a Medical Technician. After my time in the service, my husband, Gopal, joined the Army. That adventured took us to Fort Stewart, Georgia; West Point, New York; and finally, to San Antonio, Texas.

Charlie's Doggie Boutique was inspired by my yorkipoodle, Charlie. He is full of energy and always looks forward to play time. He also enjoys socializing with other pups and their owners. I hope to bring joy to other dog owners through Charlie's Boutique products.
